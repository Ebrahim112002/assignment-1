Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives Bangladesh’s growth, empowering technology, agriculture, and sustainable development for brighter futures.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures balanced development while preserving resources for future generations.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure builds the backbone of progress, connecting communities and fostering growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment promotes inclusion and opportunities for all, driving a brighter and equitable future.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future..

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh is a small country by size, but its potential is limitless. It is not the land that defines us—it is the spirit of our people, their resilience, creativity, and determination. Even within our geographical constraints, we have shown the world how innovation and unity can transform challenges into opportunities. The size of a country may remain constant, but its capacity to inspire and create change knows no bounds."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
